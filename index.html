PenPath

Cursive Learning App

PenPath is a gamified cursive learning application designed for K-5 students to develop handwriting skills through structured lessons, level progression, and interactive rewards. Inspired by platforms like Duolingo, the app combines educational content with badges, mascot-based motivation, and a handwriting scan feature to create an engaging and accessible learning experience.

My Role: UI/UX Designer & Documentation

  • Designed high-fidelity wireframes and prototypes in Figma to create an intuitive, child-friendly interface.
  • Structured lesson flows, level progression, and badge reward systems.
  • Conducted UI testing to ensure consistency between design prototypes and implemented React/React Native components.
  • Prioritized accessibility and clarity tailored for young learners.

Plate Up

A Food Waste App

PlateUP is a user-centered mobile app designed to reduce food waste by connecting individuals with surplus food to those in need. The app allows users to share, donate, and track excess food, promoting sustainability and community support.

My Role: UI/UX Designer

  • Conducted user research to understand food-sharing behaviors.
  • Created wireframes and prototypes to design an intuitive, user-friendly interface.
  • Collaborated with the project manager and design team to align app features with user needs.
  • Focused on accessibility, ensuring seamless navigation and clear call-to-actions.

One Page Landing Website

The Cozy Cat Cafe is a fictional local coffee shop website designed to create a warm, inviting online presence. The site highlights the cafe's unique concept blending a love for coffee with the charm of adoptable cats—while ensuring an engaging and user-friendly experience.
